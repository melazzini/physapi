◆ agnSolidAngle()

phys_float agn::agnSolidAngle ( phys_float  zenith,
phys_float  dz 
)
inline

Get the total solid angle of an agn angular portion.

The total solid angle consists of both parts over the equatorial horizontal plane and under the same plane. The total solid angle is defined by the zenith angle \( \theta \) and the angular interval \( \delta \theta \). Therefore, if for example the zenith is 0 and the angular interval Pi/2 then you will get \( 4 \pi \).
