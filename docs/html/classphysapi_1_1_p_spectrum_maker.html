Detailed Description

template<phys_size energyIntervals, phys_size precision = 1'000'000>
class physapi::PSpectrumMaker< energyIntervals, precision >

This is a functor class for making radiation spectra.

Template Parameters
energyIntervalsnumber of energy intervals
precisionprecision in the generation of photons. The more precision the more exact number of photons you will obtain.

A radiation spectrum is defined in our api by the next formula:

\[ \frac{dN}{dE} = \beta * N_{Tot} * \phi(E) \]

The formula gives the number of photons per energy interval corresponding to the energy \( E \).

Where \( \beta \) is the normalization parameter, \( N_{Tot} \) the total amount of photons in the spectrum and \( \phi(E) \) is the spectral formula.

We normalize this such that:

\[ \beta * \int_{E_{low}}^{E_{upp}} \phi(E) = 1 \]

and therefore:

\[ \int_{E_{low}}^{E_{upp}} dN = N_{Tot} \]

Where \( E_{low} \) and \( E_{upp} \) are the energy bounds of the spectrum.
