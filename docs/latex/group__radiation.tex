{\itshape Generic} spectrum data type. 

A t\+\_\+spectrum is an std\+::array of std\+::map$<$phys\+\_\+float,phys\+\_\+float$>$ where the first key is the energy value of the interval and the value of the std\+::map is the generic spectrum value in that interval. The std\+::array index is the index of the corresponding energy interval.

A generic spectrum may be a flux, a flux per energy interval, a photon distribution, etc.
