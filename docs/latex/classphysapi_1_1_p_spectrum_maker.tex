This is a functor class for making radiation spectra. 


\begin{DoxyTemplParams}{Template Parameters}

{\em precision} & precision in the generation of photons. The more precision the more exact number of photons you will obtain.\\

A radiation spectrum is defined in our api by the next formula\+:

\[ \frac{dN}{dE} = \beta * N_{Tot} * \phi(E) \]

The formula gives the number of photons per energy interval corresponding to the energy $ E $.

Where $ \beta $ and $ \frac{dN}{dE} = \beta * N_{Tot} * \phi(E) $ is the normalization parameter, $ N_{Tot} $ the total amount of photons in the spectrum and $ \phi(E) $ is the spectral formula.

We normalize this such that\+: \begin{DoxyVerb} \f$ \beta * \int_{E_{low}}^{E_{upp}} \phi(E) = 1 \f$
\end{DoxyVerb}


and therefore\+: \begin{DoxyVerb} \f$ \int_{E_{low}}^{E_{upp}} dN = N_{Tot} \f$
\end{DoxyVerb}


Where $ E_{low} $ and $ E_{upp} $ are the energy bounds of the spectrum.
